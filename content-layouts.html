Overview

Layout Types

  • Single-column content
  • Two-column content sections
  • Card grids
  • Hero plus supporting content

Best Practices

  • Use grids for related content groups.
  • Keep reading areas focused.
  • Use consistent spacing between sections.
  • Stack layouts on smaller screens.
