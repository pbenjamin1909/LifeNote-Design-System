Overview

Typefaces

  • Poppins: Headings and major section titles
  • Inter: Body text, labels, buttons, and UI copy

Best Practices

  • Use clear hierarchy from page title to body text.
  • Keep paragraph text readable and spaced out.
  • Avoid using too many type styles at once.
  • Use labels for small supporting UI text.
